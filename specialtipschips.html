Special tips for Chips Product

Don't judge their look but taste it first


Bawang chips have the special tips in the way of baking and shape.
  • Amount of onion need to average regarding to the our baking budget.
  • In order to making the line and shape of bawang chips, it needs 2 layer which first layer for onion and second layer regular flour.

  • Simpul kasih need the original serunding which is from meat or chicken
  • In fact, simpul kasih no need to fold strongly so the chips will be not hard to eat.

  • To get a crispy rempeyek, we need to aware the structure of batter and try to fried in 2 piece. From that, we can recognize either the batter of rempeyek are too thick or not. If the batter are thick, we have to put some water.
  • Otherwise, if the batter are too thin, we have to add a little flour in the batter.
  • The small rempeyek is good when sprinkled with anchovies and nuts.
